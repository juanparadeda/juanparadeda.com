About me

Started my career in IT as a Network Engineer in 2008. In 2010 and 2014 I obtained the CCNA and CCNP R&S certifications.

In 2020 and 2021, I began my journey to the software development world. Took training in HTML, CSS, SASS, Javascript, React.js and React Native. Also obtained Scrum Master and Scrum with Kanban certifications PSM I and PSK I

In 2024, I obtained the PMP certification by PMI. Started to explore the freelance game development landscape and launched my first demo available in Steam store!
