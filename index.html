About me

A troubleshooting conference call is a bunch of people with different skills saying “it’s not my end” to each other. That’s where I come in and steer the conversation away from a defensive mode, towards a constructive mode. In 2020 I obtained a Scrum Master certification by Certiprof and I am also getting into HTML5, CSS and vanilla Javascript frontend development being this web-resume my very first project.

I live in the city of Buenos Aires, Argentina. Spanish is my native tongue, and I am bilingual in English. I have extensive experience working on global projects with teams in Asia, Europe, North and South America, shifting around timezones, cultures, and accents.
